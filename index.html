We are in partnership with the South African Podcaster Guild

The South African Podcasters Guild (SAPG) is a growing community of local podcasters committed to raising the standard and impact of podcasting across the country. Whether you're just starting out or running a professional show, the Guild offers support, resources, and connection through workshops, events, and shared knowledge. With a strong focus on collaboration, ethical content creation, and accessibility, SAPG empowers creators to grow their audiences, reach new opportunities, and contribute to a thriving, diverse podcasting industry in South Africa.
