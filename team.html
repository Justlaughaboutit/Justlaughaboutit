Meet the Team

Lesego

Lesego Tevin Mthembu was born on the 8th of April, 1994, and raised in the vibrant heart of Soweto, Johannesburg. From a young age, Lesego demonstrated a strong sense of responsibility and a deep connection to his community. He began his formal education at Rutegang Primary School in Jabavu, where he quickly became an active and enthusiastic participant in school activities. As a member of the scholar patrol, he helped ensure the safety of fellow students, a role that reflected his dependable and caring nature even as a child. Beyond his leadership responsibilities, Lesego embraced the cultural and sporting life of his school. He played soccer, showcasing not only athletic talent but also teamwork and dedication. He also participated in gumboot dance a proudly South African tradition, which deepened his appreciation for rhythm, culture, and the expressive power of movement. These early experiences laid the foundation for his well-rounded personality and creative spirit. A passionate lover of music, Lesego has cultivated a diverse taste in genres reflecting his open-mindedness and appreciation for storytelling through sound. He is especially drawn to the soul-stirring messages of reggae, as well as the raw emotion and poetic honesty of hip-hop particularly the works of Tupac Shakur and The Notorious B.I.G.Music has been both a source of inspiration and a form of reflection for him—helping him to navigate life’s challenges and celebrate its joys.

Sabelo

Hailing from the vibrant heart of Soweto, brings a unique blend of street smarts, humor, and heartfelt storytelling to every episode. With a natural gift for turning everyday moments into laugh-out-loud anecdotes, he’s the friend you didn’t know you needed in your earbuds. Growing up in a place rich with culture and resilience, his perspective is raw, real, and always relatable. Whether he's joking about daily struggles or reflecting on life’s deeper lessons, he’s here to help you laugh about it—because sometimes, that’s the best way to deal with it.

Thato

Thato Moleko is a creative force to be reckoned with, born 1995 in the vibrant streets of White City Jabavu, where he developed a passion for storytelling and the arts. He attended Panterosa Primary School and Tulip Secondary School, laying the foundation for his future pursuits. As a media enthusiast, Thato serves as an anchor for the Just Laugh About It podcast, where he leverages his passion for storytelling, presenting, music, and art. With a relentless drive and creative energy, Thato is fuelled by his passion for his craft, his enthusiasm for innovation, and a commitment to bringing fresh perspectives to his work.
